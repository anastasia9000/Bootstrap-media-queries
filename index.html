You Gotta Love Pugs

Dog lovers agree: they just can't get enough of the incredible breed known as pugs. They're the most "pugly" breed of them all, and they're all the better for it. They'll slobber all over you and you'll love it. In fact, many have said that a pug's saliva is 200% cleaner than the average human baby. When you're thinking of your next dog and you don't know what kind you want, consider taking on the love of a pug.
